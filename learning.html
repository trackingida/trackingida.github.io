Tracking Ida

Tracking Ida is an educational alternate reality game (ARG) inspired by the pioneering investigative journalism of Ida B. Wells in the 1890s. Players follow the example of Wellsâ€™ crusade against lynching and apply her strategies to an investigation of police and vigilante killings today.

Along the way, they solve puzzles, comb through historical archives (with the help of a phonograph), role-play as investigative journalists, and harness various forms of media to spread awareness about current issues affecting their community.
